ABOUT US

A movement built on dignity & opportunity.

PROGRAM

Menstrual Health & Dignity

← Back to Programs

Menstruation is a natural biological process, yet it remains a source of shame, exclusion and missed opportunities for millions of girls in India. She Can Foundation's flagship initiative addresses this through a comprehensive, community-driven approach.

We distribute reusable sanitary pads, hygiene kits, and run taboo-breaking workshops in schools and villages — creating safe spaces where girls can learn, ask questions, and reclaim their dignity.

What We Do

🩺
Free distribution of reusable menstrual hygiene kits to girls from low-income households
🏫
Menstrual health education workshops in schools, addressing myths and promoting hygiene
👩‍👧
Parent and community sensitisation sessions to break generational taboos
📦
Emergency pad banks in schools so no girl misses class due to lack of supplies

PROGRAM

Education Access

← Back to Programs

Education is the most powerful tool to break cycles of poverty. Yet every year, hundreds of thousands of girls in India drop out due to financial pressure, distance, or cultural barriers. She Can Foundation is committed to reversing this.

We provide scholarships, learning materials, mentorship and emotional support — from primary school to college — so no girl is ever forced out of her classroom.
